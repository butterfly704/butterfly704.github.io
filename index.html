About Us

Our Story

Welcome to Druk Delights, a culinary journey through the heart of Bhutan. Founded by a passionate Bhutanese entrepreneur, our restaurant brings the authentic flavors of Bhutan to Phuentsholing.

Inspired by the traditional Bhutan Kitchen, we use time-honored recipes passed down through generations, combined with fresh, locally-sourced ingredients to create dishes that are both comforting and exciting.

Our mission is to share the warmth of Bhutanese hospitality and the unique taste of our national cuisine with both locals and visitors alike.
